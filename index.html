About Me

Hi! I am a fellow plant enjoyer who enjoys collecting, propagating, and appreciating plants.

My Favorite Plants

My current favorites are pothos, ficus (rubber plants), and pink princess philodendrons. They all have a pretty pink aesthetic and/or add a natural touch of green to a room.

Care Tips

A common mistake people make is not doing enough research on their plant. Not all the plants are the same and prefer different environments, soil, humidity, light, etc. Figure out what your specific plant needs!
